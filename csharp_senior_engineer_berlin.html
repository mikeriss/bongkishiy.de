Job Description

Be part of our journey to secure the most critical infrastructure facilities in the most convenient way.

ZipKey enables companies worldwide to wow their visitors with a fully automated and highly secure access management system. With cutting-edge technology at every layer, we challenge the established security procedures of organizations of every size. ZipKey empowers internationally renowned companies who desire to maintain a highly secure infrastructure; e.g. e-shelter, the biggest German data center company.

Your responsibilities

  • Developing, improving, and maintaining the ZipKey Windows Kiosk application (WPF / UWP)
  • Creating a beautiful, highly secure, and easy-to-use user interface for guests to check in
  • Building a user verification system using an ID scanner and a 3D camera
  • Optimizing the UX on a large touch-screen and all the additional hardware
  • Working together with hardware manufacturers to create the best solution
  • Remote fleet management & software lifecycle management for the kiosk
  • Being part of the ZipKey team and contributing to the whole product

Minimum Qualifications

  • 2+ years of professional C# & .NET development
  • Expert knowledge of .NET Framework, WCF, and either WPF or UWP
  • Great knowledge of developing & improving smooth and efficient user interfaces
  • Good understanding of how to implement external hardware with their SDKs
  • Good knowledge of the Windows ecosystem and willingness to dig deep into it

Beneficial Qualifications

  • Experience with microservice architectures / inter-process communication (WCF)
  • Deploying, maintaining, and managing distributed systems
  • Experience with JavaScript, Typescript, React, React Native, React Native Windows
  • Creating installation bundles, fleet management, and remote device management
  • Visitor management, user identification systems, or know-your-customer solutions

Other Details

  • Work type: Permanent Employee
  • Start: ASAP
  • Length: permanent
  • Workload: 40 hours per Week
  • Location: Berlin (remote days negotiable)
  • Language: German or perfect Englisch

Why ZipKey?

  • Be part of a determined team and work from the fast-growing PropTech hub in Europe: Berlin
  • Get insights into a global startup with investors and customers from all around the world
  • Become an expert in the high-security tech space with responsibility from the get-go
  • Learn from insights from all other portfolio companies in the Anyon environment
